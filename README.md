2. Addition of two 16 bit numbers
3. Subtraction of two 8 bit numbers<br>
4.Subtraction of two 16 bit numbers<br>
5.Multiplication of two 8 bit numbers<br>
6. Multiplication of two 16 bit numbers<br>
